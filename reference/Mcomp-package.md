# Data from the M-competitions

The 1001 time series from the M-competition (Makridakis et al. 1982),
and the 3003 time series and forecasts from the IJF-M3 competition
(Makridakis and Hibon, 2000).

## Source

<http://forecasters.org/resources/time-series-data/m3-competition/>.
